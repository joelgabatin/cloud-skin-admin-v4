Purchase Order Status Guide

Understanding what each status means in your purchase order workflow.
Draft
Draft
The purchase order has been created and saved but not yet approved or sent to the supplier. You can still fully edit all details, add or remove items, and change quantities and prices.
Pending Approval
Pending Approval
The PO has been submitted and is waiting for an admin to review and approve it. Line items and totals can still be returned to draft if changes are needed.
Approved
Approved
The admin has reviewed and approved this purchase order. It is ready to be sent to the supplier. Line items and costs are now locked — only the expected delivery date and notes can still be updated.
Sent
Sent
The supplier has been notified of this order. The PO is now in the supplier's hands — await the delivery and use Receive Goods when items arrive.
Partially Received
Partially Received
Some items from this order have arrived and been recorded into inventory. The remaining items are still outstanding. You can continue receiving goods or close the PO short if the remaining items will not be delivered.
Completed
Completed
All ordered items have been fully received and added to inventory. This purchase order is successfully fulfilled and closed. No further actions available.
Closed Short
Closed Short
This PO was closed by agreement with the supplier before all items were received. Items already received remain in inventory. Outstanding items have been written off. This cannot be undone.
Cancelled
Cancelled
This purchase order was voided before any goods were received. No inventory was updated. Cancellation is only possible on Draft, Approved, and Sent purchase orders.
Once a Purchase Order reaches Partially Received or beyond, it cannot be cancelled. Use Close Short to finalize a PO where remaining goods will not arrive.
