LED THERAPY TREATMENT CONSENT

Specific to LED phototherapy treatment.

What is LED Therapy?

LED Therapy uses light wavelengths to stimulate skin regeneration, reduce inflammation, and improve overall skin tone. It is non-invasive and painless.

Pre-Treatment Instructions

Arrive with clean, makeup-free skin. Avoid retinoids, acids, and exfoliants for 48 hours before treatment.

Post-Care Obligations

Avoid direct sun exposure for 24 hours. Apply SPF 50 daily. Do not use harsh skincare products for 48 hours post-treatment.

Risks Acknowledgment

Mild temporary redness, dryness, or sensitivity may occur. Rare reactions include skin irritation or pigmentation changes.
