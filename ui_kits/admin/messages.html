Message Status Guide

What each status means in your inbox.
Open
New or unresolved message requiring your attention. Every new message starts as Open automatically.
Replied
You have sent at least one reply. Conversation still active — returns to Open if the sender responds again.
Assigned
Handed to a specific staff member to handle. Still active but now their responsibility.
Closed
Fully resolved. No further action needed. Can be reopened anytime if the sender follows up.
Spam
Flagged as junk. Hidden from the main inbox and auto-deleted after 30 days. Move to Inbox if marked by mistake.
Hover over any status dot in your inbox to see a quick description. Click the dot to change the status directly.
